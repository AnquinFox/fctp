## The Payment Revolution

China is **overwhelmingly cashless**. Mobile payments (WeChat Pay and Alipay) handle **over 80% of all transactions**. As a foreigner, setting up mobile payment is your **#1 priority** after arrival.

---

## 📱 WeChat Pay (Recommended to Start Here)

WeChat Pay is China's most popular payment app, used by **over 1.3 billion people**.

### Step-by-Step Setup:

1. **Download WeChat** from your app store (search "微信")
2. **Register** with your phone number (international numbers accepted)
3. **Go to Me → Pay → Wallet**
4. **Tap "Bank Card" → Add a new card**
5. **Enter your foreign Visa/Mastercard details**
6. **Verify** via SMS code
7. **Done!** You can now pay at most merchants

### What You Can Pay For:

| ✅ Works | ❌ Doesn't Work |
|----------|----------------|
| Restaurants & cafes | International transfers |
| Convenience stores | Sending money to friends |
| Taxis & Didi | Topping up savings |
| Metro (in most cities) | Some government fees |
| Small shops & vendors | Very few things! |
| Online shopping (Taobao) | |

### Limits & Fees:

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Single transaction** | ¥4,000 RMB (~$550 USD) |
| **Daily limit** | ¥10,000 RMB (~$1,380 USD) |
| **Monthly limit** | ¥20,000 RMB (~$2,760 USD) |
| **Foreign card fee** | ~3% per transaction |
| **Supported cards** | Visa, Mastercard, JCB, Discover, Diners Club |

## 💰 Alipay (The Other Giant)

Alipay (支付宝) is equally powerful, especially for **online shopping** and **larger merchants**.

### Step-by-Step Setup:

1. **Download Alipay** (search "支付宝")
2. **Register** with phone number
3. **Go to Me → Bank Cards**
4. **Add your foreign card** (Visa/Mastercard)
5. **Complete verification**
6. **Start paying!**

### Alipay vs WeChat Pay:

| Feature | WeChat Pay | Alipay |
|---------|------------|--------|
| **Social integration** | Built into WeChat | Separate app |
| **International cards** | ✅ Supported | ✅ Supported |
| **Mini programs** | ✅ Lots | ✅ Lots |
| **Bill splitting** | ✅ Easy | ✅ Available |
| **Online shopping** | WeChat Store | Taobao/Tmall (better) |
| **Transport** | Metro/bus | Metro/bus |
| **Daily spending** | ¥10,000 | ¥10,000 |
| **Best for** | Social + daily payments | Shopping + utility bills |

> 💡 **Pro tip**: Install **both** apps. Some merchants prefer one over the other.

## 💵 Cash (Still Accepted but Rare)

Cash is still legal tender, but many merchants **prefer not to handle it**.

### When You Might Need Cash:

- Very small vendors in rural areas
- Some street food stalls
- Emergency backup when phone dies

### Where to Get Cash:

| Method | Pros | Cons |
|--------|------|------|
| **ATMs** | Widest availability | Small daily limit (~¥2,500) |
| **Airport exchange** | Convenient | Bad rates |
| **Bank exchange** | Best rates | Needs passport, queue |
| **Hotel exchange** | Convenient | Mediocre rates |

### ATM Tips:

- Look for ATMs with **Visa/Mastercard/UnionPay** logos
- **Daily withdrawal limit**: ~¥2,500-5,000 RMB
- **Fees**: Your home bank + Chinese bank both charge (~$3-5 each)
- **Best ATMs**: Bank of China, ICBC, HSBC (accept international cards most reliably)

## 🏪 Setting Up In-Store Payments

### How to Pay at a Store:

1. **Open WeChat Pay or Alipay**
2. **Tap "Scan"** (扫)
3. **Scan the merchant's QR code** (usually at the counter)
4. **Enter the amount** (or it's pre-entered)
5. **Confirm payment** with Face ID/fingerprint
6. **Done!** The merchant gets notified instantly

### QR Code Placement:

| Where | What It Looks Like |
|-------|-------------------|
| **Counter** | Printed QR code on stand |
| **Receipt** | QR code printed on receipt |
| **Screen** | Merchant shows QR on their phone |
| **Table sticker** | Sticker on restaurant tables |

## 🔧 Troubleshooting Common Issues

### "Card Not Supported"

- Try **adding the card again** (sometimes first attempt fails)
- Call your bank to **enable international transactions**
- Try a **different card** (Visa works better than Mastercard in China)
- Use **Alipay** instead of WeChat Pay (sometimes one works when the other doesn't)

### "Verification Failed"

- Make sure your **phone number** is correct
- Check if your bank sent a **verification SMS**
- Try **calling your bank** to whitelist Chinese transactions
- Some banks require you to **activate international payments** before traveling

### "Transaction Declined"

- Check your **daily limit** hasn't been reached
- Verify your **card balance** is sufficient
- Some merchants have **minimum amounts** (usually ¥1-5)
- Try a **different payment method** (switch between WeChat/Alipay)

### "Can't Add Card"

- Ensure your app is **updated to the latest version**
- Try **restarting the app** after adding card details
- Clear the **app cache** and try again
- Contact **WeChat/Alipay support** through the app

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Set up both WeChat Pay AND Alipay** — some merchants prefer one
2. **Keep a small cash reserve** (¥500-1000) for emergencies
3. **Notify your bank** before traveling to avoid card blocks
4. **Check your daily limits** — they reset at midnight China time
5. **Use the app's "Help" section** — both apps have excellent English support
6. **Scan QR codes carefully** — only scan from trusted merchants
7. **Take screenshots** of your QR code setup — helpful if you need to re-add cards
