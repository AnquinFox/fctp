# 🌐 Internet & VPN Guide for Foreigners in China (2026)

![WiFi Connection](/images/general/internet-wifi.webp)

## The Great Firewall of China

China has the world's most sophisticated internet censorship system, known as the **Great Firewall (GFW)**. Many popular websites and apps are **blocked** in China.

## 📶 WiFi in China

### Hotel WiFi:
- Most hotels offer **free WiFi** (sometimes slow)
- May require **room number + passport** to connect
- Not always reliable for video calls

### Café WiFi:
- Starbucks, McDonald's, and chain cafes usually have **free WiFi**
- May require **Chinese phone number** to authenticate
- Download a **WiFi finder app** (like WiFi Master Key)

### Airport WiFi:
- Available in most major airports
- Usually requires **passport scan** to connect
- Good for quick emails, not for streaming
