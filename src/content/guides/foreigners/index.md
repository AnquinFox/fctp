## 💡 Top 10 Tips for First-Time Visitors

1. **Download WeChat & Alipay before arrival** — you can't function without them
2. **Get an eSIM** — for internet access without a Chinese phone number
3. **Learn to use Amap** — Google Maps doesn't work in China
4. **Keep your passport with you** — needed for hotels, trains, and random checks
5. **Don't tap water** — drink bottled water only
6. **Learn basic Chinese phrases** — English isn't widely spoken outside hotels
7. **Use Didi for taxis** — more reliable than street hailing
8. **Save emergency numbers** — 110 (police), 120 (ambulance)
9. **Be patient** — cultural differences take time to adjust
10. **Have fun!** — China is an incredible country to explore
