### 馃彅Longji Rice Terraces



![Longji Rice Terraces cascading down the mountainside](/images/guilin/guilin_longji_rice_terraces_mountains_001.webp)



This is a half-day trip (at minimum) from Yangshuo. The Longji (Dragon's Backbone) Rice Terraces are about 2 hours north?absolutely worth the drive.



The terraces were built by the Zhuang and Yao ethnic minorities starting 650 years ago. They cover the mountains like giant staircases, filled with water that reflects the sky. In spring (May-June), the water-filled terraces catch the sunset light. In autumn (September-October), they turn golden with ripe rice.
