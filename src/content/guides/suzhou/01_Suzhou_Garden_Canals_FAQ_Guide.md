## 馃挕 Practical Tips



| Topic | Info |

|-------|------|

| **Best time** | March-May (spring blossoms) or September-October (mild, clear) |

| **Book gardens** | Ticketmaster app or scan QR at gate. Zhuozheng sells out on weekends |

| **Audio guide** | 楼20-40 per garden. Worth it?otherwise you're just looking at rocks |

| **Silk** | Suzhou is China's silk capital. Buy at the Silk Museum shop, not street vendors |

| **Language** | Limited English. Have destinations written in Chinese |

| **Pingjiang Road** | 1.6 km of canal-side cafes, shops, and tea houses. Best at dusk |

| **Cold Mountain Temple** | Made famous by Zhang Ji's poem "a night mooring by the maple bridge." The bell is rung 108 times on New Year's Eve
