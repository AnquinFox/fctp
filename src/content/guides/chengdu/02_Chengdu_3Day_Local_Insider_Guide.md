## 馃彅Area 4: Day Trips from Chengdu

### Leshan Giant Buddha

![Leshan Giant Buddha carved into the cliff face](/images/chengdu/chengdu_leshan_buddha_river_cliff_009.webp)

A 71-meter-tall Buddha carved into a cliff. It took 90 years to build (713-803 AD). When you're standing at its feet looking up, your brain struggles to process the scale. This is one of those rare places that's more impressive than the photos.

**Two ways to see it:**
1. **Walk down the stairway**You go from the top (head level) down to the feet. The queue can be 2 hours on weekends. Go at 8 AM.
2. **Boat cruise**You see the full Buddha from the river. 楼70, 30 minutes. No queue, better photo.

High-speed train from Chengdu East: 1 hour, 楼54.

![Emei Mountain temple architecture with clouds](/images/chengdu/chengdu_emei_mountain_temple_clouds_010.webp)

### Mount Emei

One of the Four Sacred Buddhist Mountains of China. If you're a serious hiker, the 2-day summit trek is incredible. If you're normal, take the cable car up and hike down 2 hours. The temple at the top still has monks, incense smoke, and clouds at your feet.

Train from Chengdu: 1.5 hours.
