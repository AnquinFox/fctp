## 馃 Pro Tips Nobody Tells You



1. **The park ticket lasts 4 days.** Enter at 4 PM on Day 0 (free shuttle, no queues)?do some short trail?come back fresh Day 1

2. **Mist is actually better than clear sky.** Yes really. The floating mountain effect only works when there's mist in the valleys

3. **The monkeys are NOT friendly.** Don't hold food in your hand. They will snatch it. Including from inside your bag.

4. **Food in Wulingyuan** is tourist-priced but acceptable. Try the **Three Pots** (??a local meat stew. 楼60-80

5. **Baofeng Lake** is not worth the 楼96 ticket. Skip it

6. **Yangjiajie** is the best hike in the park. The "Great Wall of Zhangjiajie" along the cliff edge is unreal. Half the tourists of Yuanjiajie

7. **Download offline maps**?phone signal is spotty in the park
