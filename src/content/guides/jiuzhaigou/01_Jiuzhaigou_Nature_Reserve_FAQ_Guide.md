# Jiuzhaigou National Park -- A Complete FAQ Guide



> July 2026 | Nature lovers / Photographers / Families | 2-3 Days



---



Jiuzhaigou, which translates to "Nine Village Valley," is one of those places that makes you question reality. The water is turquoise blue. Not a little bit turquoise?*impossibly* blue, like someone poured industrial dye into a series of alpine lakes.



It sits in northern Sichuan, at the edge of the Tibetan Plateau. Nine Tibetan villages dot the valley, 2,000 to 4,500 metres altitude. The park is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.



No embellishment needed. Just facts.

### The Ones You Cannot Miss



#### Nuorilang Waterfall (鈺咁伌)



The widest high-elevation waterfall in China (320m wide, 24m drop). It's at the central junction?you'll pass it multiple times. Go at 10 AM when the light hits the mist just right and you'll see rainbows.



#### Five Flower Lake (霉?



![Jiuzhaigou colorful turquoise lake with forest](/images/jiuzhaigou/jiuzhaigou_colorful_lake_turquoise_water_forest_001.webp)



This is *the* Jiuzhaigou photo. The water is so clear you can see submerged tree trunks 20 metres down, covered in calcium carbonate deposits that look like coral. The lake's colour changes through the day?teal at dawn, emerald at noon, sapphire in late afternoon.



**Photo spot:** Walk to the boardwalk on the east side. The tour groups all take photos from the west. Your photos will be better.



#### Five Color Pond (效?



![Jiuzhaigou colorful pool with crystal clear water](/images/jiuzhaigou/jiuzhaigou_waterfall_forest_scenic_park_nature_003.webp)



The smallest lake in the park but the most intensely coloured. In autumn, with red and yellow leaves reflected on the turquoise surface, it's almost too beautiful to be real. Early morning is best, before the wind creates ripples.



####Long Lake (鈧?



![Daocheng Yading snow peak](/images/jiuzhaigou/jiuzhaigou_daocheng_yading_snow_peak_meadow_006.webp)



The highest and deepest lake in the park (4,300m elevation, 80m deep). Surrounded by snow-capped peaks. The path from the shuttle stop is wheelchair accessible. Take the mandatory photo, then walk 10 minutes along the ridge for the *real* photo.



#### Pearl Shoal (顭?



A wide, shallow cascade over a calcareous tufa bed. The water looks like thousands of pearls rolling downhill. This is where the 1986 version of *Journey to the West* was filmed (Chinese viewers will recognise it instantly).

##What Nobody Tells You



1. **Altitude sickness is real.** The park ranges from 2,000m to 4,500m. On Day 1, you'll feel it?shortness of breath, mild headache. Take it slow. Don't drink alcohol.

2. **The queues are real too.** At peak October, expect 30-60 min queues for shuttle buses at the entrance. Go at 7 AM.

3. **Don't feed the birds or monkeys.** They're aggressive. I saw a monkey snatch a GoPro from someone's hand.

4. **The parking lot at the entrance is a scam magnet.** Ignore anyone offering "skip the queue" tickets. Only buy at the official ticket window.

5. **The park closes early.** Last entry 2 PM. Last shuttle leaves 5 PM. You do not want to miss the last shuttle.

6. **Bring toilet paper.** Public toilets in the park don't supply it.

7. **Some trails close in winter.** Check before you book.
